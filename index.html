About Us

Our tradition of best service

10 Years of welcoming hospitality….

We’ve been expecting you! We know what it means to come from a long and tiring trip and to long for a genuine welcome, a good drink and a comfortable bed to stretch out on. If you enjoy being made a fuss of, you’ve come to the right place!

Families and business people love us, too. We’ve been hosts to countless college graduations, family vacations, re-unions, birthday celebrations, meetings and training programs.

Our appeal to such a wide range of clientele might have to do with our attention to detail, making it all very personal and also our terrific location:

Right around the corner from Faro airport, minutes away from iconic places such as Vilamoura, Vale do Lobo and Q. do Lago, Gida Apartments offer easy access to amazing beaches, aquaparks, golf clubs and resorts.

Come on in. We’re here to make you feel good!
